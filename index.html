PIG DICE

Rules of game:

  • A player repeatedly rolls die untill either 1 is rolled or player decides to hold.
  • If a player chooses to hold then it is next players turn and their total is added to their score.
  • Any number other than 1 is added to the total.
  • First player to reach 100 wins!
